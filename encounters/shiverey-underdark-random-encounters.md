# Shyverey Underdark Random Encounters

Random low/mid encounters for the chardalyn expedition with T'ha, Tena, Bro Kar'Ku-Sha, and Shael'Vir Tarren.

Import `homebrew/shiverey-underdark-bestiary.json` into 5e.tools to get Shael and the d8 random encounter table. The JSON files in `encounters/` are quick Encounter Builder starting points with fixed counts.

| d8 | Encounter | Default combat | Note |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Veil of Darkmantles | 5 darkmantles | Ceiling ambush; attacks the light source. |
| 2 | Ringing Scree Vein | 1 grick + 5 piercers | Loud sounds trigger a cave-in hazard. |
| 3 | Duergar Appraisal Patrol | 4 duergar | Can become social before it becomes steel. |
| 4 | Kuo-toa Shrine Post | 6 kuo-toa + 1 kuo-toa whip | Can be bypassed with a convincing omen. |
| 5 | Blind Hunters in an Echo Tunnel | 8 grimlocks | Silence matters more than light. |
| 6 | Hook Horror Creche | 2 hook horrors | They defend a nest; can be detoured. |
| 7 | Watchful Fungus | 4 violet fungi + 2 drow reskinned as spore servants | Heat and blood wake the fungus. |
| 8 | Wrong Knocking in the Stone | 1 wounded umber hulk | Start at about 60% hit points. |

## Shael at the table

Shael is not meant to outshine the party. Use her for route-reading, warning signs, silent tactical support, and occasional emergency damage. Her `Silent Command` and `Agent of the Circle` features are the main ways to keep her useful without turning her into the protagonist with knives.
